Toffeedoodle Cookie Recipe

Description: A soft-baked snickerdoodle cookie enhanced with toffee bits.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups plus 2 tablespoons Imperial Sugar Extra Fine Granulated Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 3/4 cups all-purpose flour*
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup toffee bits
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Preheat one to 350Â°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside. 1
  2. Cream butter and 1 1/2 cups sugar in bowl of an electric mixer until light and fluffy, about 5 minutes. Add vanilla and eggs. Slowly add flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Mix until combined.2
  3. Reduce speed to low and add toffee bits.3
  4. In a small bowl combine remaining sugar and cinnamon. Use a tablespoon scoop to portion cookies and roll into a ball. Coat dough in cinnamon sugar. Place on prepared sheets, 2-inches apart. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes. Let cool on pans for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
